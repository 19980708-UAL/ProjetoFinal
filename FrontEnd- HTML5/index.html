KofKofCovid Login

Nowadays, society faces challenges that test all the knowledge acquired and obliges us all, as beings belonging to the same ecosystem to work in perfect harmony.

Recently, one of these challenges emerged in the form of a pandemic, which was given the name COVID-19.

In the face of these challenges, this application was created, supported by Artificial Intelligence with the aim of providing the global community with a non-invasive form of personal pre-screening, anywhere and anytime, through which a person can record and make upload a sound from yourself or someone else and check with the indicated degree of accuracy if you have COVID-19.
